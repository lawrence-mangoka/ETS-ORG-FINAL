Major areas of Study

1.Theological studies

Biblical Theology, Doctrine of God, Doctrine of the Word of God, Doctrine of Christ, Doctrine of Man, Soteriology, Ecclesiology, and Eschatology

2.Old Testament Courses

Genesis, Exodus to Deuteronomy, historical books, poetic and wisdom literature. Old Testament prophets.

3.New Testament Courses

Synoptic Gospels, Gospel of John, Acts, Pauline letters, General letters, Hebrew and Revelation.

4.Historical Theology

Church History 1, Church History 2 and Reformation Church History.

5.Practical Theology

Missions, leadership, worship, Biblical ethics, hermeneutics, preaching, marriage and family, and counseling.
